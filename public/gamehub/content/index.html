Youth Rite of Passage • Gate 1

The Signal Path

Orin must deliver a market parcel through a noisy city, resist distractions, catch scatterbugs, and follow the glowing path to the First Gate.

Lesson: What you give your attention to becomes part of you.

  • Stay on mission.
  • Notice distractions.
  • Use Quiet Listen to refocus.
  • Finish the path and earn the Path Stone.

How to Play

Controls

  • Move: Arrow Keys / WASD
  • Interact: Space / Enter
  • Quiet Listen: Q

Mission Flow

  1. Deliver the parcel to Mara before time runs out.
  2. Catch 3 Scatterbugs causing confusion.
  3. Follow the glowing signal path to the Gate.
