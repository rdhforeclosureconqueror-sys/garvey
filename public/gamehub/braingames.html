Freeze Runner
A neon runner moves through a cyber maze. When the alert flashes, you must stop instantly or the spider sentinel locks onto you.
What this trains
Inhibition, stop-signal control, and resisting impulsive movement when conditions suddenly change.
How to use it
Press Start. Let the runner move. When FREEZE appears, press the Freeze button immediately and do not move until the alert clears.
Design note
I made each game feel like a mini sci-fi mission so it feels more like a real game and less like a worksheet.
