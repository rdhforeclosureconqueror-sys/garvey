# Tap CRM MVP Scope

## Included in MVP

- Feature-flagged Tap CRM extension namespace
- Isolated Tap CRM APIs under `/api/tap-crm`
- Public/customer and owner-facing Tap CRM surfaces (phased)
- Tag-based resolution flow (phased)
- Config/template/module-driven behavior (phased)

## Explicitly Excluded from MVP

- Full messaging/campaign automation
- AI recommendations
- Full payment processor replacement
- Deep GARVEY workflow fusion

## Phase 1 Notes

Phase 1 establishes domain boundaries, tenancy, permissions, and dashboard isolation strategy only. Data models and functional Tap Hub/Tag flows are deferred.
