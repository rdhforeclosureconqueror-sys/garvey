## 8. Known limitations

- Manual browser QA is still required before release signoff.
- Voice quality may vary by browser fallback voice, operating system, locale, and installed speech engines.
- Open-ended writing validation is rule-based; it is not full semantic grading and should be treated as coaching/feedback support rather than an exhaustive writing score.
- Renderer-specific limitation: automated coverage verifies renderer existence, package usage, structural markup, and absence of placeholder-only output in Node; it does not prove all viewport sizes, fonts, and device-specific wrapping behaviors.
- Audio-generation/caching limitation: cached AI audio depends on the upstream TTS service, token configuration, writable cache directory, and network availability; browser speech fallback must remain enabled for service failures.

- [ ] Desktop: open the Grade 4, Grade 5, and Grade 6 hub views and launch representative English packages.
